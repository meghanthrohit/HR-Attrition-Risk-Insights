# HR Attrition Risk & Employee Retention Insights

## Project Overview
This project analyzes employee attrition data to identify high-risk employees and key factors influencing employee turnover.

## Objectives
- Identify employees at risk of attrition
- Analyze salary, overtime, satisfaction, and experience impact
- Help HR teams take proactive retention actions

## Tools Used
- Python (Pandas, Matplotlib)
- Power BI
- Business Analytics Techniques

## Key Insights
- Employees with low salary and overtime have higher attrition risk
- Early-tenure employees are more likely to leave
- Attrition risk increases with poor work-life balance
